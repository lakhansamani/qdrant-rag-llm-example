EMPLOYEE ONBOARDING GUIDE
==========================
Version: 4.2 | Last Updated: 2025-01

1. FIRST DAY CHECKLIST
-----------------------
- Collect your MacBook from the IT desk (Building A, Floor 2).
- Log into Okta (SSO) using your company email. Okta manages all app access.
- Enable MFA on your Okta account — this is mandatory before you can access anything else.
- Join Slack: #general, #engineering, #your-team channels.
- Request system access via the IT portal at it.company.internal.
- Meet your onboarding buddy — they're assigned by HR and will help your first week.

2. TOOLS & ACCOUNTS
--------------------
- Slack: primary communication. Use threads. DMs discouraged for technical topics.
- Jira: project and issue tracking. Your team lead will add you to the board.
- GitHub: source code. Request access to the org via IT portal. Use your company email.
- Notion: internal wiki, meeting notes, RFCs. Read-only by default, ask for write access.
- Figma: design files. Engineering has viewer access.
- 1Password: password manager. IT will send an invite. Use it for all credentials.
- Zoom: video calls. SSO login via Okta.

3. ENGINEERING ONBOARDING (Week 1)
------------------------------------
- Day 1: Setup dev environment. Run the setup.sh script in the engineering-setup repo.
- Day 2: Read the architecture overview in Notion → Engineering → Architecture.
- Day 3: Pick a "good first issue" from your team's Jira board and submit your first PR.
- Day 4: Attend the weekly engineering sync (Thursdays, 10am CET).
- Day 5: Pair program with your buddy on a real task.

4. PTO & LEAVE POLICY
-----------------------
- Annual leave: 25 days per year. Prorated for joiners.
- Sick leave: unlimited, no approval needed for < 3 days. Doctor's note required for > 3 days.
- Request PTO via Workday at least 2 weeks in advance.
- Public holidays follow your country of residence.
- Parental leave: 16 weeks fully paid for primary caregiver, 8 weeks for secondary.

5. BENEFITS
-----------
- Health insurance: covered 100% by company. Dental and vision included.
- Learning budget: €2,000/year for courses, books, conferences.
- Home office budget: €1,000 one-time allowance for new joiners.
- Equipment refresh: every 3 years.
- Mental health: 12 free therapy sessions per year via Nilo Health.

6. ENGINEERING NORMS
---------------------
- On-call: engineers rotate on-call every 6 weeks. You join after your 3-month probation.
- Code reviews should be done within 24 hours. Be kind and constructive.
- If you're blocked for > 1 hour, ask for help. Don't suffer in silence.
- Postmortems are blameless. Focus on systems, not individuals.
- Documentation is part of the definition of done — if it's not documented, it's not done.
